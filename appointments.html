Appointments

Each piece is custom made to order and because of the cost of labour, we are unable to offer any refunds or exchanges once the piece has been started.

The deposit (50%) will be used to fulfil the order. This deposit covers the materials used to process your order. Because we are outsourcing the casting, orders will take up to 2-3 weeks. We do not use diamonds, or add colours to grillz and we do not make grillz for pre-molars or molars. If you have any underlying dental issues (especially gum problems), please see your dentist beforehand. If you have any other questions, please email picassagrillz@gmail.com

Once your completed form is received, we will be in contact to set an appointment and to discuss your order.
